Global Earthquake/FaultLines Data Map (Updated Daily)


Below is a map of the world and displays all earthquake events for the day that the map is accessed. Data is updated daily by the United States Geological Survey (USGS).

Circle size and color indicate the magnitude of the earthquake where the bigger the circle size and redder the color, the more intense the earthquake's magnitude is.

The map is made using the Leaflet.js. The timeline controls are courtesy of Leaflet.timeline 1.2.0
